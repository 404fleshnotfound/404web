about.

a shithead coder, tattooer, artist. junky for brutalist design, futurism, and love.

the flesh protocol and encrypted sigilism in gen. was birthed with the idea that sometimes less is more.

but also more is more.

here's the lore.

born and raised in nyc. i grew up listening to punk, started coding at 11, was making art way before then and get my heartbroken at 14.

was an activist for, still am, just more of an internet robinhood now - i guess. was a corpo for a while, quit. play shows to fucking no one. adore every minute of it.

get popular on the internet for a while. it fucking sucked, it was fun, it fucked me up.

realize that within society right now, true artistry is impossible. so i drop off the face of the fucking planet.

im bouncing around the country for a little while. reading, writing, writing songs, ruminating.

i knew i didn't want to run the rat race, i didn't want to chase temporary. i fucking hate "content" and AI and all the fucking bs

so i slow way down, go back to my roots. move back home, making punk music in my childhood room.

i start tattooing, start coding again, never stopped making art.

i start to realize that the internet is a tool, and i'd like to use it to spread a better ideology not built around doom.

now i'm here.

tattooing is real different, it's raw, it's intimate. it's heavy.

but there's a lot of love, a lot of trust, a lot of feeling and that's why i make art.

bc if ur not feeling, wtf are u doing?
